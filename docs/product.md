# PRODUCT SPECIFICATION

Dzinr is a highly focused visual micro-feedback platform built for architects, digital creators, and interface designers.

## Problem Statement
Designers have plenty of public catalog sites (Dribbble, Behance) but lack crisp, quantitative micro-feedback regarding specific design files. Long threads of comments are slow to read and rarely translate to actionable ratings.

## Solution: The Swipe Interface
Dzinr translates design evaluation into a gamified, mobile-first swiping loop:
- **Upload**: Drop raw designs, renderings, mockups, or blueprints directly into the app.
- **Micro-swipe**: The community swipes left to reject/dislike, right to approve/like, or taps to submit micro-reviews.
- **Algorithmic Scoring**: Designs receive a dynamic design score compiled from swipe velocities, category tags, and relative peer feedback.
- **Refinement Grid**: The creator updates design slides in response to feedback loops, monitoring score change over time.
